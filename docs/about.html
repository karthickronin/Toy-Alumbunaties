Toy Alumbunaties began with a simple belief: every child deserves to experience pure joy and wonder. Founded by Deepak and Jeeva, both graduates in Business Administration specializing in Shipping and Logistics, we started with a passion for bringing smiles to children's faces.

What started as entertaining kids at family gatherings has grown into a professional children's entertainment service. We've had the privilege of performing at hundreds of birthday parties, school events, and family celebrations, each one filled with laughter and magical moments.

Our approach is simple: we connect, we entertain, and we create memories. Every child is unique, and we believe their special day should be filled with wonder, laughter, and unforgettable fun experiences.
